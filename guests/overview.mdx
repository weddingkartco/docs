Guest management is the foundation of every wedding on Weddingkart. You add guests, organize them into groups, send targeted WhatsApp announcements, and track every response — all from a single place.

## How guest management works

The workflow follows four steps:

1. **Add guests** — manually one at a time, or by importing an Excel or CSV file
2. **Organize into groups** — segment guests by side, family, event, or any criteria you need
3. **Send targeted announcements** — send WhatsApp messages to one or more groups
4. **Track responses** — monitor delivery, RSVP, travel ticket, and ID card status per guest

## What you can do

**Add guests your way**

Add guests by hand for small lists, or import hundreds at once from an Excel or CSV file. Weddingkart reads your existing spreadsheet columns and maps them automatically.

**Auto-create groups from Excel columns**

When you import an Excel file with columns like "Brideside" or "Groomside" filled with Yes/No values, Weddingkart creates those groups automatically. You don't need to build groups separately.

**Store custom attributes per guest**

Attach any custom data to a guest — hotel name, pickup point, meal preference, bus number. These attributes can be used as placeholders in WhatsApp messages so each guest receives a message with their own specific details.

**Track guest status at a glance**

For each guest you can see:

- **Delivery status** — whether their last message was sent, delivered, or read
- **RSVP status** — attending, not attending, or no response
- **Travel ticket status** — collected or pending
- **ID card status** — collected or pending

**Export to Excel or Google Sheets**

Export your full guest list with all collected data to Excel or sync it to Google Sheets. Weddingkart can create a new sheet for you, sync into your existing sheet, or fill a copy of your original Excel file and return it to you.
