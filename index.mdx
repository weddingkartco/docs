## How Weddingkart works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up with your phone number and verify via OTP on WhatsApp or SMS. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your wedding">
    Create a wedding event with the couple's names, date, and venue. You can manage multiple weddings from a single account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your guest list">
    Add guests manually or import them from an Excel file. Weddingkart automatically creates groups based on your columns (Brideside, Groomside, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send announcements">
    Pick a template, write your message, select recipients, and send — or schedule for later. Guests receive a personal WhatsApp message directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
