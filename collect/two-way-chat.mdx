---
title: "Two-Way Chat"
description: "Reply to individual guest messages directly from the Weddingkart app."
---

When a guest replies to any WhatsApp message from your wedding channel, their message appears in your Weddingkart inbox. You can respond from the app, and the guest receives your reply as a WhatsApp message — without leaving Weddingkart.

Guests always stay on WhatsApp. Only you and your team operate from the Weddingkart app.

## How it works

1. You send a WhatsApp announcement to your guests (an invite, RSVP request, or event update).
2. A guest types a reply — for example, "What time should I arrive?" or "Can I bring a \+1?"
3. Their message appears in your **Inbox** inside Weddingkart.
4. You type a reply in the app.
5. The guest receives your message on WhatsApp.

The conversation continues back and forth with no friction on either side.

## Access the inbox

Tap **Inbox** or **Chat** in the Weddingkart app. All guest conversations are listed there, organised by guest. You can see the full message history for each conversation and reply directly from the same screen.

## Key features

- **Real-time messages** — all team members on your wedding account see incoming guest messages as they arrive.
- **Per-guest conversations** — messages are organised by guest, so you can see the full thread with each person.
- **Message history** — conversations are retained for the duration of the wedding, so nothing gets lost.
- **Read receipts** — you can see when your message has been delivered and read.

## Use cases

Two-way chat handles individual guest needs that bulk announcements can't address:

- Answering questions about venue address, event timing, or parking
- Following up personally with guests who haven't RSVPed
- Coordinating accommodation or transport details with specific guests
- Responding to guests who have a special request or dietary requirements

<Tip>
  Two-way chat works alongside bulk announcements — you can broadcast the same message to all guests and then handle individual follow-up questions in the inbox without switching tools.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Two-way chat is included in the **Standard plan**. It is not available in the Basic plan.
</Note>
