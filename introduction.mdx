Weddingkart is an all-in-one wedding management platform built for professional event managers. It gives you a single place to manage every guest interaction — from the first invite through RSVPs, travel coordination, and ID collection — across weddings of any size.

## The problem it solves

Large weddings involve hundreds of guests, multiple families, and logistics that span weeks. Coordinating over WhatsApp groups, tracking RSVPs in spreadsheets, and chasing travel details by hand eats up hours that should go into running a great event.

Weddingkart replaces that chaos with a structured workflow: you manage everything from one app, and guests interact through a channel they already use every day.

## How guests experience it

Your guests don't need to download an app, create an account, or open any link. Every interaction — invites, RSVPs, travel ticket requests, ID collection — happens directly on WhatsApp. Guests receive a message, tap a button or send a file, and they're done.

Messages are sent through the official WhatsApp Business API, so guests see a verified sender ("Wedding Invite") with high deliverability.

<Note>
  Weddingkart is designed for professional event managers, not the couple themselves. If you manage weddings for clients, this platform is built for you.
</Note>
