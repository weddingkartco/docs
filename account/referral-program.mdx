---
title: "Referral Program"
description: Earn free WhatsApp credits by referring other event managers to Weddingkart
---

The referral program lets you earn bonus WhatsApp credits by sharing Weddingkart with other wedding planners and event managers. When someone signs up using your referral link and purchases a wedding plan, you receive credits added directly to your account.

## What you earn

You receive **1,000 bonus credits** for each successful referral — when your referred contact signs up and makes their first purchase. The person you refer also receives **1,000 credits** as a joining bonus when they sign up.

There is no limit to the number of referrals you can make. Every paying referral adds more credits to your account.

<Tip>
The more event managers you refer who become paying customers, the more free messages you accumulate — significantly reducing your per-wedding cost over time.
</Tip>

## How to refer someone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Referral section">
    Open the Weddingkart app and navigate to the **Referral** section from the drawer menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy your referral link">
    Copy your unique referral link from the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with a fellow event manager">
    Send the link via WhatsApp, email, or any other channel. A direct message works better than just posting a link publicly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits are added automatically">
    When your contact signs up and makes a purchase, your 1,000 bonus credits are added to your account automatically. No manual claim required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using your referral credits

Referral credits are stored in your personal Weddingkart account and are not linked to any wedding by default. Before you can use them, you need to transfer credits from your personal account to a specific wedding.

To use referral credits:

1. Open the **Refer & Earn** section from the drawer menu to check your current credit balance.
2. Transfer credits from your personal account to the wedding where you want to use them.
3. Once transferred, the credits appear in the **Wallet** section of that wedding and can be used for sending WhatsApp announcements.

<Note>
You can only transfer referral credits to weddings that you have created. Credits cannot be withdrawn as cash or transferred to another user's account.
</Note>

Referral credits follow the same per-country WhatsApp pricing rules as purchased credits. Credits do not expire as long as your Weddingkart account is active.
